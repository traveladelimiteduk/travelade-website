Essential Requirements

What You Need for Your Visa

The essential requirements every applicant must have. We handle the rest!

Valid Passport

UK-issued passport valid for 3+ months beyond your return date with 2+ blank pages

UK Residence Proof

Valid UK residency (BRP/Visa) valid for 3+ months beyond your travel dates

Financial Proof

UK bank statements (3-6 months) showing sufficient funds for your trip

Employment Proof

Employment letter, student status, or self-employment documentation

Passport Photos

Recent passport-size photographs (35x45mm, white background)

Previous Visas

Previous Schengen visas in old passports (if any)

Document Checklist

Complete Document Requirements

We align your checklist to the lead consulate and prepare drafts and proofs for you.

Identity & UK Residence

  • Valid passport (3+ months beyond return, 2+ blank pages)
  • UK residence permit/BRP (valid 3+ months)
  • Previous Schengen visas (if any)
  • Recent passport-size photographs (35x45mm)

Financial & Employment Proof

  • UK bank statements (3-6 months)
  • Employment letter or contract
  • Recent payslips (3 months)
  • Employment details/Student Details

We Handle For You

  • Travel Insurance (€30,000+)
  • Flight reservations
  • Hotel booking proofs
  • Day-wise itinerary

Additional Documents

  • Biometrics (if not given in 59 months)
  • Sponsorship documents (if applicable)
  • Invitation letter (if visiting family)
  • Relationship proofs (family visits)
