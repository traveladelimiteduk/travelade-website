Essential Requirements

Everything you need to prepare for your Schengen visa application.

Valid Passport

Your passport must meet these requirements:

  • Minimum 6 months validity from intended return date
  • At least 2 blank pages
  • No damage or wear

Application Form

Complete visa application form:

  • Accurate information
  • Signed by applicant
  • Date and sign within 15 days of appointment

Photos

Passport photograph requirements:

  • 2 recent color photos
  • White background
  • 35x45mm size

Flight Itinerary

Travel booking proofs:

  • Round-trip flight booking
  • Can be provisional booking

Accommodation

Proof of lodging:

  • Hotel booking confirmation
  • Host invitation letter (if staying with someone)

Proof of Funds

Financial documents:

  • 3 months bank statements
  • Original bank letter

Travel Insurance

Required insurance:

  • Minimum €30,000 coverage
  • Valid for all Schengen countries
  • Cover entire stay

Cover Letter

Purpose of travel:

  • Explain travel purpose
  • Itinerary details
